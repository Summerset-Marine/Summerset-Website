Our Story

Two decades on
Wisconsin water.

Summerset Marine Construction was founded in 2001 by a team of marine construction specialists who believed that Wisconsin's lakefront property owners deserved better than what the market was offering: seasonal docks that shifted, failed, and needed to be replaced on a cycle that cost more than it should.

We set out to build the opposite: permanent structures, engineered for Wisconsin's conditions, backed by a warranty that meant something. Twenty-four years later, that commitment hasn't changed. We've grown to serve seven markets across Wisconsin's finest lakes, but the approach is the same — site by site, one well-built pier at a time.

How We Work

What we stand for.

01

Honesty over salesmanship.

We'll tell you when a seasonal dock is a better fit for your situation. We'd rather earn your trust than a project we're wrong for. Most of our business comes from referrals — that's why.

02

Engineering before aesthetics.

A beautiful pier that fails in its third winter isn't a success. We spec every post, every weld, and every anchor point for your specific lake, depth, and exposure — then we make it look right.

03

One team, start to finish.

Your project manager is your contact from the first call through the final walkthrough. We don't hand you off to a subcontractor at installation. The people who sold you the job are the people who build it.

04

Permits done right.

We've never built an unpermitted structure. It protects you, it protects us, and it keeps Wisconsin's lakes healthy for the next generation. We manage every filing in-house — no exceptions.

05

Long-term thinking.

We build things we intend to service for decades. Our warranty means something because we're still here to honor it. Every decision we make — from material selection to hire — is made with that in mind.

06

These are our lakes too.

Our team lives and recreates on the same lakes where we work. We care about their health, their clarity, and their future. That's not marketing copy — it's the reason we do this work the way we do it.
